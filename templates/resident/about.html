Our Mission

More than just a directory.

CityLink started with a simple idea: Why is it so hard to find a trusted plumber or a good local doctor? In a world of instant connection, local services remained fragmented and offline.

We set out to change that. CityLink is a smart platform that bridges the gap between residents needing help and skilled professionals seeking work.

By digitizing word-of-mouth trust through verified reviews and community vouching, we aren't just fixing sinks—we are empowering the local economy.
